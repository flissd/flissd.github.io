About Me

I am a Ph.D. student focusing on model-based image restoration tasks. I investigate the limitations of current image restoration methods, like plug-and-play, and propose modifications to boost performance. I also explore the application of modern methods for solving inverse problems with Poisson noise in low-photon count weather science data. My interests include other signal recovery applications.
